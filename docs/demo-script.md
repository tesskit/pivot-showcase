# Pivot — Demo Script

## Persona
**Name:** Ada (named after Ada Lovelace — the first computer programmer)
**Background:** HR coordinator, 8 years experience
**Gap:** 6 years out of workforce raising kids
**Situation:** Youngest just started school full time, ready to return
**Location:** Chicago, open to hybrid
**Target:** HR or HR tech roles
**Key win:** Built onboarding program from scratch — cut 3 weeks to 9 days, 30% retention improvement

---

## Turn 1 — Ada opens
> I'm Ada. I was an HR coordinator for 8 years and left 6 years ago to raise my kids. My youngest just started school full time so I'm ready to go back. My biggest win was building our entire onboarding program from scratch — cut the process from 3 weeks to 9 days and new hire retention improved 30% in the first year. I'm in Chicago, open to hybrid, and want to go back into HR or HR tech.

*Pivot responds with warm acknowledgment and one question*

---

## Turn 2 — Ada answers
> Honestly the gap on my resume feels scary. I just don't know how to explain six years away.

*Pivot addresses the gap warmly, one more question*

---

## Turn 3 — Ada signals ready, pipeline fires
> That actually helps. I'm ready — what's out there for me?

*Full pipeline runs — 60-90 seconds*
*Full response arrives: jobs, STAR story summary, email nudge*

---

## Email
Ada enters her email, hits Send ✦. The career packet arrives containing:
- STAR story
- Resume bullets
- Cover letter opening
- Quick win
- Live job listings with Apply Now buttons
